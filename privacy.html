Privacy & Cookie Policy

Analytics

We use a lightweight analytics provider (Plausible) only after you accept cookies. No personal data is collected by default. If you accept analytics, a small script will run to collect aggregated metrics like pageviews and referrers.

Third-party links

The site links to Discord and Google Drive; those services have their own privacy policies.

Managing cookies

You can withdraw consent at any time via the cookie banner; this will disable analytics loading.
